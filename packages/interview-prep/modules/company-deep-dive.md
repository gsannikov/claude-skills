# Company Deep Dive

In-depth company research for interview preparation.

## Research Dimensions

| Dimension | Sources | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Company Overview | Website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase | Know the basics |
| Recent News | Google News, TechCrunch | Current events |
| Culture & Values | Glassdoor, LinkedIn posts | Fit assessment |
| Tech Stack | Job posts, GitHub, StackShare | Technical prep |
| Team Structure | LinkedIn, org charts | Know who you'll meet |
| Financials | Crunchbase, press releases | Stability signals |
| Challenges | News, Glassdoor reviews | Interview talking points |

## Output Template

```markdown
# [Company] Deep Dive

## Quick Facts
- **Founded**: 2015
- **Size**: 500-1000 employees
- **Funding**: Series C, $150M
- **HQ**: Tel Aviv, offices in US
- **Industry**: AI/ML Infrastructure

## What They Do
[2-3 sentence summary of core business]

## Recent News (Last 3 Months)
- [Date] - [Headline + 1-line summary]
- [Date] - [Headline + 1-line summary]

## Tech Stack
- Languages: Python, Go, Rust
- Infrastructure: AWS, Kubernetes
- ML: PyTorch, custom frameworks

## Culture Signals
- **Glassdoor Rating**: 4.2/5
- **Key Themes**: Fast-paced, engineering-driven, flat hierarchy
- **Red Flags**: [Any concerns from reviews]

## Leadership
- CEO: [Name] - Background
- CTO: [Name] - Background
- Your potential manager: [Name] - Background

## Interview Talking Points
1. [Their challenge] → [How your experience addresses it]
2. [Recent news] → [Your perspective/question]
3. [Their tech] → [Your relevant experience]

## Questions to Ask
- About the role: [Specific question]
- About the team: [Specific question]
- About growth: [Specific question]
```

## Commands

| Command | Action |
|---------|--------|
| `Deep dive: [company]` | Full research report |
| `Quick brief: [company]` | 5-minute overview |
| `Research [topic] at [company]` | Focused research |

## Integration Points

### From Job Analyzer
- Pull existing company research from analysis
- Use contacts for team/manager info

### For STAR Stories
- Match company challenges to your stories
- Identify relevant experience highlights

### For Salary Negotiation
- Company financials inform leverage
- Funding stage affects comp flexibility
