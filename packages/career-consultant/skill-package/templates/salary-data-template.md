## ğŸ“ˆ Scoring Formula Components

### Base Income Score (0-20 points)

Calculate based on offer vs. your minimum:

```
If offer < minimum:
  score = 0

If offer >= minimum AND offer < target:
  score = 10 + ((offer - minimum) / (target - minimum)) * 10

If offer >= target:
  score = 20
```

### Company Tier Bonus (0-5 points)
- Tier 1: +5 points
- Tier 2: +3 points
- Tier 3: +1 point
- Tier 4: +0 points

**Total Income Score**: Base (20) + Tier Bonus (5) = **25 points max**

## ğŸ¯ Benchmarking Your Offer

### Calculate Your Percentile

**Formula**:
```
Percentile = (Offers below yours / Total offers) * 100
```

**Interpretation**:
- **< 25th percentile**: Below market, negotiate
- **25-50th percentile**: Fair but room to improve
- **50-75th percentile**: Good offer
- **75-90th percentile**: Strong offer
- **> 90th percentile**: Exceptional offer

### Red Flags in Compensation
- Offer significantly below market (-20%+)
- No equity component
- Unclear bonus structure
- "Unlimited PTO" (often means less vacation)
- Heavy weighting on variable comp
- Equity with unfavorable terms
