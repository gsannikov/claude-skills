## Style Variants

### Short & Punchy (Option 5)
- Under 280 chars
- Emoji bullets
- Clear value props
- Direct CTA

### Medium Detailed
- 300-500 chars
- More context
- Multiple CTAs
- Conversation starter

### Long-Form
- 800-1500 chars
- Full story/context
- Multiple sections
- Rich formatting

## Engagement Scoring (1-10)

| Dimension | Points | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Hook strength | 2 | Captures attention first line |
| Value clarity | 2 | Clear benefit/proposition |
| Engagement prompts | 2 | Questions, CTAs |
| Visual appeal | 2 | Emojis, formatting, media |
| Platform fit | 2 | Follows best practices |

**8-10**: High potential | **5-7**: Moderate | **1-4**: Needs revision
