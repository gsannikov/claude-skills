# Platform Specifications

## Threads (Instagram)

### Limits
- Standard: 500 chars
- With attachment: 10,000 chars
- Shows "Read more" after ~500

### Formatting
✅ Bold, italic, underline, strikethrough
✅ Emojis, bullet points, line breaks
❌ No hashtags (algorithm ignores)
❌ No clickable links in body

### Media
- Images: Up to 10
- Video: Up to 5 min
- Auto link previews

### Algorithm (2025)
1. Engagement (40%): Likes, comments, shares
2. Recency (30%): Fresh content priority
3. Interest (20%): Past interactions
4. Profile visits (10%)

### Best Practices
✅ Conversational, authentic tone
✅ Ask open-ended questions
✅ Post 1-3x daily
✅ Respond to comments within 1 hour
✅ Use visuals
❌ No corporate speak
❌ No hashtags
❌ No cross-posts from X/Instagram

### Posting Times (US)
- Weekdays: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM ET
- Weekends: 10 AM-2 PM ET

## X (Twitter)

### Limits
- Standard: 280 chars
- Premium: 25,000 chars

### Formatting
✅ Emojis, line breaks, mentions
✅ Hashtags (max 2-3)
❌ No rich text

### Media
- Images: Up to 4
- Video: 2:20 (standard), 10 min (Blue)
- GIFs: 1

### Algorithm (2025)
1. Engagement rate
2. Recency
3. Media presence
4. Authenticity

### Best Practices
✅ Front-load important info (first 100 chars)
✅ 1-2 hashtags max
✅ Include visual
✅ Use threads for detail
❌ Don't overuse hashtags
❌ Avoid link-only tweets

### Posting Times (US)
- Weekdays: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM ET
- Weekends: 9 AM-12 PM ET

## LinkedIn

### Limits
- Post: 3,000 chars
- Shows "see more" after ~140 chars
- Article: 125,000 chars

### Formatting
✅ Emojis (sparingly)
✅ Bullet points, line breaks
✅ Numbered lists
❌ No official rich text

### Media
- Images: Up to 9
- Video: Up to 10 min
- Documents: PDF

### Algorithm (2025)
1. Dwell time (reading duration)
2. Engagement
3. Relevance
4. Connection strength

### Best Practices
✅ Professional but authentic
✅ Hook in first 2 lines
✅ Tell stories, share insights
✅ Use data/statistics
✅ Post 2-5x per week
✅ 3-5 hashtags max
❌ Avoid promotional content

### Posting Times (US)
- Tue-Thu: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM ET
- Avoid weekends/evenings
