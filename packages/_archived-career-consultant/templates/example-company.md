## Interview Process

**Difficulty**: Medium
**Average Duration**: 3 weeks from application to decision
**Common Interview Types**: Coding, System Design, Behavioral, Take-Home

### Typical Process (4-5 rounds)

1. **Recruiter Screen** (30 min)
   - Background review
   - Role explanation
   - Salary expectations

2. **Technical Phone Screen** (60 min)
   - Coding challenge (LeetCode medium level)
   - System design discussion (senior roles)

3. **Take-Home Assignment** (3-4 hours)
   - Real-world problem
   - 1 week to complete
   - Optional but recommended

4. **Onsite/Virtual Interviews** (4-5 hours)
   - Coding interviews (2 rounds)
   - System design (1 round)
   - Behavioral (1 round)
   - Team fit (1 round)

5. **Final Chat** (30 min)
   - VP Engineering or CTO
   - Culture fit
   - Vision alignment

### Interview Tips
- Focus on Python and system design
- Be ready to discuss scalability
- Prepare STAR method examples
- Know their products/services
- Ask about team structure and projects
